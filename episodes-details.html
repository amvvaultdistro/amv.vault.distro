Global Distribution & Rights Management

AMV Vault provides a centralized interface for Abantu Recordings artists to manage their digital presence. Through our Supabase-backed infrastructure, we ensure your metadata is accurate and your royalty claims are verified via YouTube API integration.

Claims & Verification Process

  • Submit your YouTube Channel ID via the Profile section.
  • Receive a unique verification token generated by our secure server.
  • Update your YouTube "About" section with the token for verification.
  • Admin approval confirms your artist status and unlocks subscription plans.
