Sjava Completes 'Inkanyezi' Trilogy & Announces Nationwide Tour

Ten years after his official debut, South African icon Sjava has officially released INkanyezi NeZinkanyezi, the final chapter of his groundbreaking trilogy. The album follows a unique "fan-first" rollout where listeners became part of the creative process via live EPs recorded throughout 2025.

To celebrate a decade of storytelling, Sjava has confirmed the 10 Year Celebration Tour, kicking off at Cape Town's Grand Arena on March 21st, before heading to Durban ICC on April 11th. The project features heavyweight collaborations with Msaki, Anatii, and Jesse Clegg, cementing Sjava’s status as a star among stars.
